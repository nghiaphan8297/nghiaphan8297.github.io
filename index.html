About

I am an international student from Vietnam. Where I come from - Vietnam, doesn't offer great educations and oppotunities in art. So I decided to study abroad - I left my country at the age of fifteen.

I had high school in Massachusetts, U.S and went to Montserrat College of Art - in the same state. I'm a second-year-college-student and currently working as an animator. I also take interest in illustration and design.
